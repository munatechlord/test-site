At MEST, He is working hard to develop programming skills so that he can be an essential member of future software companies that he would build or join in the future.

He is perfectly suited in roles related to business development, fundraising as well as marketing for potential startups and established companies that require his services. He has currently written two books; “Clueless to CEO” and “Crowdfunding works in Africa”. “Clueless to CEO” describes his experience is launching and growing his first startup and gives ideas to African millenials on the best ways to build their first business. “Crowdfunding works in Africa” demystifies crowdfunding in Africa, and gives business owners and non-profit leaders the tools to build successful crowdfunding campaigns.
